### Ingredients 
1. Jamuns ( Jambul ) – 500gms 
2. Rock salt – 2 pinches 
3. Salt – 1 pinch 
4. Water – 1 glass 

### Preparation   

1. Clean the Jamuns by washing in water. 
2. Soak in water for about 1 hour.
